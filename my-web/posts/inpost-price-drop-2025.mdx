**InPost** has launched a new international shipping service connecting Poland with **France, Spain, Portugal, Italy, Belgium, the Netherlands, and Luxembourg**. This innovative solution enables both online stores and individual customers to send packages abroad quickly and conveniently – and at the lowest price on the market.

![International Shipping](https://inpost.pl/sites/default/files/2025-01/mapa-c2c.png)

---

## A New Era in International Shipments

With this service, simply drop off your package at the nearest [**Paczkomat**](https://inpost.pl/jak-odebrac-paczke-w-paczkomacie) (InPost parcel locker) in Poland, and your recipients can pick it up from a Paczkomat or a **PUDO** (Pick Up Drop Off) point in major cities such as Paris, Madrid, Brussels, Lisbon, or Rome.
---
“**Thanks to our new service, sending holiday gifts or important shipments to family and friends abroad will be exceptionally easy**! Just drop off your parcel at the nearest Paczkomat in Poland, and your loved ones can pick it up from a Paczkomat or a PUDO point in Paris, Madrid, Brussels, Lisbon, or Rome.” – Rafał Brzoska, CEO and Founder of InPost Group

## Unbeatable Pricing and Value

The service offers extremely competitive rates, starting at **28.99 PLN** for a small package up to 25 kg. For comparison:

- Sending the same package via DPD costs **99 PLN**
- And via DHL – **114 PLN** .

In addition, InPost’s new loyalty program awards quadruple InCoin rewards for each international shipment sent or received.

## Expanding Market Reach for Businesses

This new offering is an ideal solution for companies looking to tap into **7 key European markets** and reach hundreds of millions of potential customers. By leveraging this service, Polish entrepreneurs can expand their business internationally – with plans to eventually extend the service to the UK.

![International Shipping](https://inpost.pl/sites/default/files/2025-03/hero-text-en_1.png)

## Extensive Network and Infrastructure

InPost Group boasts an impressive infrastructure:

- Over **79,000 out-of-home points** as of Q3,
- Nearly **44,000 modern Paczkomat machines** and approximately **35,000 PUDO points** across 9 countries (including the UK, France, Poland, Italy, Spain, Portugal, Belgium, Luxembourg, and the Netherlands).
- In the past 12 months, a record **10,000 machines** were installed, marking a 33% year-over-year increase.

Moreover, InPost provides courier and fulfillment services for around **100,000 e-commerce sellers**, having processed **892 million parcels** in 2023.

## Commitment to Sustainability

InPost has long prioritized environmental care. The company’s decarbonization strategy is integrated with its business objectives. As one of the first in Poland, InPost joined the SBTi initiative and is committed to achieving **NET-ZERO by 2040**.
